95 GHz methanol maser survey toward 1000 BGPS sources & Catalog

We search for 95 GHz class I methanol masers toward 1020 Bolocam Galactic Plane Survey (BGPS) sources using the Purple Mountain Observatory 13.7 m radio telescope. A total of 213 sources showing methanol detections. We compared the line width of methanol and HCO+ thermal emission for all methanol detections source-by-source, and on that basis, we find that 205 of the 213 detections are very likely to be masers. This corresponds to an overall detection rate of 95 GHz methanol masers toward our BGPS sample of 20%. Of the 205 detected masers, 144 (70%) are new discoveries.
Combining our results with those of previous 95 GHz methanol maser searches, a total of 481 95 GHz methanol masers are now known. We compiled a catalog listing the locations and properties of all known 95 GHz methanol masers.
